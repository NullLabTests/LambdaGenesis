# LambdaGenesis Architecture

## Layer 1 — Symbolic Chemistry
Expressions interact as computational molecules.

## Layer 2 — Energy Economics
All structures consume energy and decay.

## Layer 3 — Mutation
Random perturbations introduce diversity.

## Layer 4 — Selection
Persistent structures survive longer.

## Layer 5 — Ecology
Interaction creates niches and competition.

## Layer 6 — Emergence
Stable symbolic organizations arise.
